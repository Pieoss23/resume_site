AI / RAG Demo concept

University PDF RAG Tutor

A simple retrieval-augmented assistant that learns from university PDFs and answers with grounded references instead of generic text.

  • PDF ingestion, chunking, embeddings, and semantic search
  • Answer flow that cites the retrieved lecture material
  • Useful for exam prep, topic recap, and quick concept lookup
Stack idea: Python, vector DB, OpenAI API, lightweight UI
Media / UX Demo concept

World Podcast Explorer

A podcast site that lets people move through categories, regions, and languages, then listen directly without getting lost in cluttered discovery flows.

  • Browse by topic, country, or publisher
  • Episode cards with player state and saved queue
  • Discovery-first layout tuned for large content catalogs
Stack idea: Next.js, search API, audio player, personalized feed
Automation Production mindset

ERP Workflow Console

A management interface for integrations, scheduled jobs, and exception handling across ERP processes where visibility matters more than decoration.

  • Job health, alerts, retries, and payload traceability
  • Operator-friendly logs with fast filtering
  • Designed for real internal usage under pressure

Data / AI Demo concept

Document Operations Assistant

A workflow tool that extracts structured information from business documents and routes it into downstream systems with a human review step where confidence is low.

  • OCR and parsing for invoices, forms, or internal files
  • Validation rules before data lands in the target system
  • Human-in-the-loop review for the risky edge cases
Stack idea: OCR pipeline, rules engine, review UI, ERP handoff
